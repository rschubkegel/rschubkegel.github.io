Official Podcast

21C Townball is proud to present The Twenty-First Century Townball Podcast, hosted by Grant More. The podcast takes a deep dive into the history of the game, its current growth, and what makes it unique. Episodes feature the players who developed the sport, giving them a chance to share their love of the game.
